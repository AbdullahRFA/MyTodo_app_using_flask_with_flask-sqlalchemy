About This ToDo App

This ToDo app is designed to help you organize and manage your tasks efficiently. You can add new tasks, update existing ones, and delete tasks you no longer need.

How to Use the App

  • Add a New Task: On the home page or click on (MyTodo), fill in the "Todo Title" and "Todo Description" fields, then click on the "Add Todo" button. Your task will be saved and displayed in the task list.
  • Update a Task: Click the "Update" button next to the task you want to modify. This will take you to an edit page where you can update the title or description. Click "Save Changes" to update the task.
  • Delete a Task: If a task is no longer needed, click the "Delete" button next to it. This will permanently remove the task from your list.
  • View All Tasks: The home page displays all tasks in the order they were added, with the most recent ones appearing at the top.

Features

  • Simple and intuitive interface for managing tasks.
  • Tasks are stored in a database for persistent storage.
  • Quick actions to add, update, and delete tasks.
